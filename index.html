Our Journey – From Simple Solutions to Everyday Essentials

Founded with a vision to make plasticware safer, smarter, and more sustainable, Manu Plast began its journey as a small-scale manufacturer with big dreams. Over the years, we’ve evolved into a trusted household name, known for our wide range of BPA-free bottles and PET jars designed for modern Indian homes.

What sets us apart is our attention to design, quality, and usability. Every Manu Plast product is a result of thoughtful innovation, practical design, and customer-centric thinking. Whether it's a fridge bottle that’s easy to grip, or a jar that keeps your kitchen neatly organized—we create products that add convenience and color to your everyday life.

Why This Matters:

We are more than just a plasticware company—we’re a team committed to eco-conscious living, customer trust, and Indian-made quality you can rely on.

Safe Materials. Trusted Quality. Total Transparency.

At Manu Plast, we use only food-grade, BPA-free plastic in every product we manufacture. Our preferred materials—PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene)—are known for their durability, safety, and recyclability.

PET

PET is lightweight, strong, crystal clear, and ideal for water bottles and food containers.

HDPE

HDPE offers excellent moisture barrier and strength—perfect for jars and packaging.

Safety is Our Priority:

Every Manu Plast product is made in a controlled, hygienic environment and undergoes strict quality testing to ensure it meets food safety standards. No harmful chemicals. No compromises.

Who We Are

Manu Plast is a leading innovator in sustainable drinkware, dedicated to crafting high-quality, eco-conscious bottles that inspire healthier lifestyles and a greener planet.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Manu Plast began with a simple mission: to reduce single-use plastic waste by creating durable, stylish, and functional eco-friendly bottles. From our headquarters in Seattle, we’ve grown into a global brand trusted by millions. Our team of designers and engineers works tirelessly to blend cutting-edge technology with sustainable materials, ensuring every Manu Plast bottle is a step toward a cleaner future.

Our Mission

To empower people to live sustainably by providing innovative, eco-friendly hydration solutions that reduce waste and promote wellness.

Our Vision

A world where single-use plastics are a thing of the past, and every individual has access to stylish, sustainable drinkware that reflects their values.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to global impact, here are the key milestones that define Manu Plast’s story in sustainable drinkware.

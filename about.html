About Manu Plast

Our Materials & Unwavering Safety Standards

At Manu Plast, safety begins with the right materials. Our products are crafted using 100% BPA-free, food-grade plastics—primarily PET and HDPE—which are known for their strength, clarity, and recyclability. These materials are used across our product lines, including water bottles, kitchen storage jars, and more.
For our premium collection, we also offer jars and containers that meet the same high standards of food safety, durability, and eco-friendliness. Each product is rigorously tested to meet Indian food-grade certifications and ensure long-lasting performance in your home.

Commitment to Sustainability & Impact

Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. By choosing Manu Plast, customers actively reduce plastic waste by switching to long-lasting, reusable bottles and jars.
Our manufacturing processes emphasize energy efficiency, low-waste production, and environmentally responsible sourcing. Additionally, we support and participate in plastic recycling awareness drives, hydration education programs, and eco-conscious partnerships that contribute to a cleaner, greener India.
We believe that impactful change starts with the small, everyday choices—and Manu Plast is here to make them easier.

Behind the Design: Innovation at Manu Plast

Every Manu Plast product is designed with purpose. Our bottles feature ergonomic grips, spill-proof lids, and sleek shapes that fit seamlessly into daily life—from school bags and car cup holders to office desks.
Our kitchen jars are space-saving, stackable, and fitted with airtight seals that lock in freshness and keep your pantry organized. Behind every product is a design philosophy that blends user experience, style, and sustainability.
We constantly listen to customer feedback, adapt to lifestyle trends, and innovate to ensure our products remain both functional and beautiful.

Our Manufacturing Process & Quality Control

Our state-of-the-art facility in Vasai, Maharashtra operates with a focus on efficiency, hygiene, and sustainability. From raw material handling to final packaging, every step is closely monitored to ensure that only top-quality products reach your home.
We use energy-efficient machines and maintain strict quality checks at every stage. Sustainability isn’t just in the product—it’s in how we make it.

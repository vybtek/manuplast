Who We Are

AquaVita is a leading innovator in sustainable drinkware, dedicated to crafting high-quality, eco-conscious bottles that inspire healthier lifestyles and a greener planet.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, AquaVita began with a simple mission: to reduce single-use plastic waste by creating durable, stylish, and functional bottles. From our headquarters in Seattle, we’ve grown into a global brand trusted by millions. Our team of designers and engineers works tirelessly to blend cutting-edge technology with sustainable materials, ensuring every AquaVita bottle is a step toward a cleaner future.

Our Mission

To empower people to live sustainably by providing innovative, eco-friendly hydration solutions that reduce waste and promote wellness.

Our Vision

A world where single-use plastics are a thing of the past, and every individual has access to stylish, sustainable drinkware that reflects their values.
